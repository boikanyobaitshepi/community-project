Drug Addiction Support

If you or someone you know is struggling with drug addiction, there are resources available to help. Here are some support groups, counseling services, and rehabilitation center:

Support Groups

  • Lets talk: Meetings held every Monday and Thursday at 6 PM, located at House no 10 Lenatong section. Contact: 079 382 4406.
  • SMART Recovery: Online and in-person meetings available. Visit their website for more information: smartrecovery.org.

Counseling Services

  • Hope Counseling Center: Offering individual and group counseling for substance abuse. Contact: 083067804771.
  • Community Health Clinic: Free counseling services available for individuals struggling with addiction. Contact:014 566 4720.

Rehabilitation Centers

  • Recovery Haven: Residential treatment program for drug addiction. Located at E46 Masosobane Phokeng. Contact:079 775 9372 .
  • New Beginnings Rehab: Outpatient treatment services for substance abuse. Contact: 064 781 6050.
